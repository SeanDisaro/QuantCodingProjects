Nvidia (NVDA) plans to host a private summit next week with startups focused on solving data center power problems that could hold up the development of artificial intelligence, according to several people who were invited, The Information’s Anissa Gardizy reports. Executives from power and electrical engineering startups are expected to attend the summit, including companies in which Nvidia has taken equity stakes, the author notes.Published first on TheFly – the ultimate source for real-time, market-moving breaking financial news.
