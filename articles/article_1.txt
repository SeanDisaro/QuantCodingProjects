Nvidia (NVDA) and Deutsche Telekom (DTEGY) are getting ready to announce plans for a EUR1B data center in Germany as part of broader efforts to establish more AI infrastructure across Europe, Bloomberg’s Eyk Henning, Jillian Deutsch, and Christina Kyriasoglou report. Both firms will contribute funding for the $1.2B project, with SAP (SAP) serving as a customer of the facility, the authors say, citing people familiar with the plans.Published first on TheFly – the ultimate source for real-time, market-moving breaking financial news.
