AI is reshaping the tech sector, fueling investment in semiconductors, cloud computing, and software. While individual AI stocks can be rewarding, they also carry higher risk. For long-term investors seeking diversified exposure, Vanguard ETFs offer broad market coverage with exposure to leading AI companies. Using the TipRanks’ Best Vanguard ETFs tool, we’ve identified three standout funds; Vanguard Information Technology ETF (VGT), Vanguard S&P 500 Growth Index Fund ETF (VOOG), and Vanguard Mega Cap Growth Index Fund ETF (MGK), that are loaded with AI-focused tech stocks that could support long-term portfolio growth.Let’s take a look at these ETFs in detail.Vanguard Information Technology ETF is focused solely on U.S. technology stocks, with major holdings including Nvidia (NVDA), Apple (AAPL), Microsoft (MSFT), Broadcom (AVGO), and Palantir Technologies (PLTR). The ETF provides pure exposure to the tech sector, making it appealing for investors targeting strong growth potential.
