Broadcom (AVGO) that the company is working to bring the latest NVIDIA (NVDA) AI technology to VMware Cloud Foundation. These broad options for NVIDIA accelerated computing address the need for increased choice, flexibility, and operational efficiency for AI infrastructure, enabling VCF customers to harness the full potential of NVIDIA’s AI innovation.
