Nvidia’s (NVDA) plan to invest up to $100B in OpenAI has stalled after some inside the chip company expressed doubts about the deal, The Wall Street Journal’s Berber Jin and Robbie Whelan report, citing people familiar with the matter. Now, the two sides are rethinking the future of their partnership, some of the people said.
