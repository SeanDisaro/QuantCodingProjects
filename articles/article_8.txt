Nvidia’s (NVDA) plan to invest $100B in OpenAI to help it run its AI models has stalled after some have expressed doubts about the deal, Berber Jin and Robbie Whelan of The Wall Street Journal reports, citing people familiar with the matter. Nvidia Chief Executive Jensen Huang has privately emphasized in recent months that the original $100B agreement was nonbinding and not finalized, while criticizing what he described as a lack of discipline in OpenAI’s business approach and concern over competition, the sources added.Published first on TheFly – the ultimate source for real-time, market-moving breaking financial news.
