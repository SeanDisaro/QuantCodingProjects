Universal Music Group (UMGNF) announced a collaboration with NVIDIA (NVDA) to pioneer responsible AI for music discovery, creation, and engagement leveraging NVIDIA AI infrastructure and UMG’s music catalog comprising millions of tracks covering every genre and era. UMG is collaborating with NVIDIA to enrich and enhance the music experience of the billions of music consumers worldwide who enjoy the world’s most popular form of entertainment.
