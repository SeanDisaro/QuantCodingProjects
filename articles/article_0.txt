Supermicro (SMCI) announced the broad availability of its Nvidia (NVDA) Blackwell Ultra solutions. Supermicro is now delivering Plug-and-Play-ready Nvidia HGX B300 systems and GB300 NVL72 racks to customers worldwide. Blackwell Ultra’s GB300 and B300 can be configured to use up to 1400W per GPU while offering 50% greater inferencing performance while utilizing FP4 compute and 50% more HBM3e capacity compared to NVIDIA Blackwell. Having larger memory sizes and faster inferencing speeds is crucial for running more complex and powerful models efficiently.
