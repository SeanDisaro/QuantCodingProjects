From 2026 to 2028, Microsoft plans to invest more than $1B in cloud and AI infrastructure and ongoing operations across Thailand. The investment is the centerpiece of Microsoft’s Advancing National Growth, Prosperity, and Global Competitiveness with AI initiative, supporting trusted and inclusive access to cloud and AI technology while helping skill millions of Thai citizens across every sector of the economy. “As the cloud and AI become central to economic growth and national competitiveness, countries need both access to world-class technology and the know-how to put it to work,” said Brad Smith, Microsoft Vice Chair and President. “Microsoft is proud to support Thailand’s vision for secure cloud and AI, combining trusted infrastructure, secure platforms, and skills so AI adoption translates into real competitiveness and job creation.”
