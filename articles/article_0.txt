Wedbush’s top analyst Daniel Ives is impressed by Palantir Technologies’ (PLTR) recent AI partnerships. He views Palantir’s strategy of integrating data, software, and AI tools as “growth catalysts” as companies increasingly embed AI into their essential operations.
