Nvidia (NVDA) stock has fallen 3.8% last week amid AI overspending fears, sparked by a key investor pulling $10 billion from Oracle’s (ORCL) Michigan data center project. Growth stocks tumbled this month on the Federal Reserve’s “wait-and-watch” stance, yet NVDA stock is up 29.7% so far this year. Wall Street’s top analysts continue to remain bullish on Nvidia’s long-term prospects, forecasting a 50% increase in NVDA stock price over the next twelve months.On TipRanks, NVDA stock commands a Strong Buy consensus rating based on 39 Buys and one Hold rating from top analysts. Also, the average Nvidia price target of $261.31 signals huge upside from today’s $174 levels.
