Advanced Micro Devices (AMD) stock rallied on Wednesday alongside news that the company plans to refresh its Ryzen line with updated CPUs. A new leak claims that the company is working on Ryzen 7 9750X and Ryzen 5 9650X variants for its Zen 5 CPUs. X user chi11eddog, who has a solid record with AMD rumors, is the source of these leaks.Here’s what the leak claims about these new AMD CPUs.The latest leak suggests that these are simply refreshes of AMD’s Ryzen 7 9700X and Ryzen 5 9600X. The biggest difference between those and the new CPUs in the leaks is TDP. AMD has reportedly boosted the TDP for these CPUs to 120 W to get more power out of them.
		AMD stock was up 1.8% on Wednesday but was still down 6.47% year-to-date. The shares have also rallied 84.8% over the past 12 months.Today’s Ryzen refresh leak isn’t the only news that has pushed AMD stock higher. The semiconductor company has also reached an agreement with Samsung (SSNLF) to help it better compete with Nvidia (NVDA) in the AI sector.Turning to Wall Street, the analysts’ consensus rating for AMD is Moderate Buy, based on 21 Buy and eight Hold ratings over the past three months.
