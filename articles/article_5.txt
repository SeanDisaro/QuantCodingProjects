At its AIPCon 9 conference, Palantir Technologies (PLTR) announced several major partnerships with GE Aerospace (GE), Ondas (ONDS), NVIDIA (NVDA), Centrus Energy (LEU), and LG CNS. These announcements focus on integrating Palantir’s Artificial Intelligence Platform (AIP) across defense, energy, manufacturing, and AI infrastructure sectors. Following the news, PLTR stock was up 2% on Thursday.Here are the highlights from the new partnerships:NVIDIA: Palantir and NVIDIA are teaming up to deliver “sovereign AI operating system reference architecture.” This brings together NVIDIA’s Blackwell Ultra systems with Palantir’s AIP to provide a complete, production-ready AI data center solution that lets organizations keep full control over their data, models, and applications.
		GE Aerospace: Expanding their existing partnership, GE Aerospace and Palantir are using “agentic AI” to enhance military aircraft readiness for the U.S. Air Force, specifically focusing on the J85 engine that powers the T-38 training fleet. This includes optimizing supply chains, manufacturing, and maintenance.Ondas Holdings: Palantir partnered with Ondas and World View to build a “next-generation multi-domain intelligence platform.” The partnership combines Ondas’s autonomous drone systems and World View’s high-altitude stratospheric balloons with Palantir AIP to boost intelligence, surveillance, and reconnaissance (ISR) for defense customers.Centrus Energy: Centrus is partnering with Palantir to drive cost savings and operational efficiencies as it expands U.S. uranium enrichment capacity.LG CNS: LG CNS and PLTR expanded their partnership to accelerate AI transformation across the LG Group. This formalizes their plan to bring stronger, AI‑driven business impact across LG’s wide range of companies.Overall, these deals indicate that the companies are turning to Palantir to bring their data together, scale AI, and modernize critical operations. From aircraft and drones to nuclear fuel, AI infrastructure, and manufacturing, Palantir’s software is becoming a core tool for companies moving toward digital and AI‑driven operations.
