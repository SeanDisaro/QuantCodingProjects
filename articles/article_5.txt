Shares in tech giant Alphabet (GOOGL) are only up about 8% year-to-date, with growth tempered by the recent selloff in the elite Magnificent Seven group. However, its Google Cloud unit on Wednesday pulled back the curtains on a raft of new AI-focused partnerships at the ongoing Google Cloud Next 2026 conference in Las Vegas.Turning to Wall Street, Alphabet’s shares remain a Strong Buy based on analysts’ consensus rating. This breaks down to 26 Buys and five Holds assigned by 31 analysts over the past three months.In addition, the average GOOGL price target of $387.68 implies about 14% upside from current trading levels.
