Cisco (CSCO) unveiled the Silicon One G300, a 102.4 Tbps switching silicon is says is designed for massive AI cluster buildouts. “The Cisco Silicon One G300 will power new Cisco N9000 and Cisco 8000 systems that push the frontier of AI networking in the data center. The systems feature innovative liquid cooling and support high-density optics to achieve new efficiency benchmarks and ensure customers get the most out of their GPU investments. In addition, the company enhanced Nexus One to make it easier for enterprises to operate their AI networks – on-premises or in the cloud – removing the complexity that can hold organizations back from scaling AI data centers,” the company stated.Published first on TheFly – the ultimate source for real-time, market-moving breaking financial news.
