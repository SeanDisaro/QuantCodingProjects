Nvidia (NVDA) CEO Jensen Huang said he hopes China will allow the company to sell its H200 AI chips in the country and the license is being finalized, Wen-Yee Lee and Ben Blanchard of Reuters reports. “The H200, the actual license for H200 is being finalized. And I’m hoping that also the Chinese government would allow Nvidia to sell the H200, so they have to decide.
