Advanced Micro Devices (AMD) stock was up on Friday even after the chipmaker’s shares were hit with a lackluster analyst update. Five-star Bernstein analyst Stacy Rasgon assigned the company a Hold rating with a $235 price target, which represents a potential 18.5% upside for the shares.Rasgon’s neutral stance on AMD stock isn’t too surprising. The analyst has long had a Hold rating for AMD shares, largely due to his belief that the artificial intelligence (AI) boom has overvalued the company. Even so, the analyst did recently comment that it was surprising AMD experienced such a sharp drop following its earnings report last month.Rasgon is no stranger to the semiconductor space.
