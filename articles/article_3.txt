HPE (HPE) announced its AI Grid, saying it is built on the Nvidia (NVDA) reference architecture to connect AI factories and distributed inference cluster. “The HPE AI Grid enables service providers to deploy and operate thousands of distributed inference sites, turning AI installations into a single intelligent system,” the company said in a statement. It added, “The HPE AI Grid solution, part of Nvidia AI Computing by HPE portfolio, delivers predictable, ultra-low latency performance at scale for real-time AI services, zero-touch provisioning, and automated security with integrated orchestration.”  Published first on TheFly – the ultimate source for real-time, market-moving breaking financial news.
