Catch up on the top artificial intelligence news and commentary by Wall Street analysts on publicly traded companies in the space with this daily recap compiled by The Fly. BIG INVESTMENT: Sequoia is set to make a “big investment” in Anthropic, the AI startup behind chatbot Claude, George Hammond of The Financial Times reports. The group is joining a funding round led by GIC and Coatue, which are contributing $1.5B each, multiple sources told the Times. Microsoft (MSFT) and Nvidia (NVDA) have already committed to invest up to $15B in total in the company. FUNDING TALKS: AI voice-generator startup ElevenLabs is in discussions for new funding that would nearly double its valuation to $11B within months, the Financial Times’ Ivan Levingston and George Hammond report. The company is seeking to raise hundreds of millions of dollars from investors, with talks coming just four months after a secondary share sale valued it at $6.6B, the authors say, citing people familiar with the matter. 
		GROWTH ROUND: DealHub.io announced a $100M growth round led by Riverwood Capital. The investment will be used to accelerate global expansion and advance the company’s Agentic Revenue Hub for the AI era, the company said. “Enterprises are entering a new era, where revenue execution must be autonomous, adaptive, and continuously optimized,” said Eyal Elbahary, CEO of DealHub.io. “This investment enables us to push the boundaries of what Agentic Quote-to-Revenue can deliver, enabling enterprises to operationalize revenue strategies with unprecedented intelligence and control.” “DealHub is addressing a fundamental challenge enterprises face as the pace of innovation drives revenue ecosystems to evolve,” said Jeff Parks, Co-Founder & Managing Partner at Riverwood Capital. “DealHub’s modern, adaptable platform is equipped to support the speed, flexibility, and intelligence required to scale efficiently in the age of AI. We are excited to partner with Eyal and the team and believe DealHub has decisively re-architected how revenue execution operates across the enterprise.”DAMAGES REQUEST: Elon Musk wants OpenAI and Microsoft to pay him between $79B-$134B in damages over his claims the AI company defrauded him by abandoning its nonprofit roots, Robert Burnson of Bloomberg reports, citing a court filed damages request. 2026 LAUNCH: Nvidia is growing its data center business while advancing personal and edge AI offerings, with a roadmap that extends from the current N1 and N1X platforms to the next-generation N2 and N2X systems, DigiTimes’ Monica Chen and Sherri Wang report. According to the report, Nvidia is targeting a 2026 launch for a Windows on Arm notebook. DEVELOPER REQUESTS: Internal Google (GOOGL) data shows Gemini API calls surged from about 35B in March 2025 to roughly 85B in August 2025, The Information’s Erin Woo reports.
