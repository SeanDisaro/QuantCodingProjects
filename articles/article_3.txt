Nvidia (NVDA) “must live with” the licensing terms on sales of its second most advanced AI chip to China, Commerce Department Secretary Howard Lutnick said at a hearing, Reuters’  David Lawder and Alexandra Alper report. “The license terms are very detailed. They’ve been worked out together with the State Department, and those terms Nvidia must live with,” he said.
