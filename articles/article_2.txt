U.S. lawmaker John Moolenaar asked Commerce Secretary Howard Lutnick to explain President Trump’s decision to allow Nvidia (NVDA) to sell its H200 chips to China, Stephen Nellis of Reuters reports. “As AI evolves, aggregate computing power – not theoretical per-chip efficiency – will remain the engine of progress,” Moolenaar wrote in a letter to Lutnick. “Approving the sale of cutting-edge chips to Chinese companies risks undercutting the extraordinary strategic advantage that President Trump achieved in his first term.”Published first on TheFly – the ultimate source for real-time, market-moving breaking financial news.
