AI inference startup Baseten has raised $300M at a $5B valuation in a round led by venture capital firm IVP and Alphabet’s (GOOG) (GOOGL) CapitalG, with participation from Nvidia (NVDA), Kate Clark of The Wall Street Journal reports, citing people familiar with the matter. In particular, Nvidia is investing $150M into the startup as part of the deal, which more than doubles the startup’s valuation, Clark notes.Published first on TheFly – the ultimate source for real-time, market-moving breaking financial news.
